Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about contacting admin and application process

What is the best way to contact admin?

Email is preferred for most inquiries at admin@city-ihl.ca. For urgent matters, call during office hours.

How long does it take to get a response?

We aim to respond within 1-2 business days. Application acknowledgements are sent within 24 hours.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

Include: full name, contact info, programme of interest, question details, and relevant background information.

Can I schedule an appointment with admin?

Yes, contact admin@city-ihl.ca to schedule appointments during office hours, available in-person, by phone, or video conference.
