A Successful 2nd Conference on Economic Growth in Atlantic Canada

May 17, 2025 Dieppe, New Brunswick

From May 15 to 17, 2025, City Institute of Higher Learning had the pleasure of hosting the 2nd Conference on Economic Growth in Atlantic Canada. Under the theme "Innovation Hubs: Fostering Entrepreneurship and Global Cooperation," the event brought together business leaders, researchers, policymakers, and community members to share ideas, experiences, and visions for the economic future of the region.

Over the course of three days, participants attended inspiring keynote sessions, engaged in hands-on workshops, and took part in dynamic networking events. Together, they explored new opportunities for collaboration and innovation to support inclusive and sustainable growth across Atlantic provinces.
