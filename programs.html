Executive Diploma in Alternative Dispute Resolution

1 Year Diploma

A comprehensive one-year programme designed to equip participants with in-depth knowledge, practical skills, and a strong theoretical foundation in various methods of resolving disputes outside traditional litigation.

Format On-campus/Virtual
Language English, French
Credits 30 ECTS

Programme Overview

This comprehensive programme provides in-depth training in Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) methods.

Curriculum
  • Introduction to Alternative Dispute Resolution
  • Mediation Principles and Practices
  • Arbitration Procedures and Techniques
  • Negotiation Strategies
  • Conciliation and Facilitation
  • Legal Frameworks for ADR
  • Ethics in Dispute Resolution
  • Case Studies and Practical Applications
Learning Outcomes
  • Master various ADR techniques and methodologies
  • Develop practical conflict resolution skills
  • Understand legal and ethical considerations in ADR
  • Apply ADR principles in real-world scenarios
  • Facilitate negotiations and mediations effectively
Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue careers as mediators, arbitrators, conflict resolution consultants, legal advisors specializing in ADR, and human resources professionals.

Admission Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent
  • Minimum 2 years of professional experience
  • Proficiency in English or French
  • Statement of purpose
  • Two letters of recommendation
Tuition Fees

Full programme: $4,500 CAD (Canadian Dollars). Payment plans and scholarships available.

An advanced programme with research component for those seeking comprehensive knowledge in dispute resolution methods with a focus on mediation, arbitration, negotiation, and conciliation.

Format On-campus/Virtual
Language English, French
Credits 60 ECTS

Programme Overview

This advanced diploma programme combines rigorous academic study with practical training in alternative dispute resolution.

Curriculum
  • Advanced Theories of Conflict Resolution
  • Research Methods in Social Sciences
  • Advanced Mediation and Arbitration
  • International Dispute Resolution
  • Comparative ADR Systems
  • Dispute Resolution in Specialized Contexts
  • Thesis Research and Writing
  • Professional Practice and Ethics
Learning Outcomes
  • Develop advanced research skills in ADR
  • Analyze and evaluate ADR systems and practices
  • Design and implement ADR programs
  • Conduct independent research in dispute resolution
  • Apply advanced ADR techniques in complex situations
Career Opportunities

Graduates are prepared for leadership roles as senior mediators, arbitrators, ADR program directors, and dispute resolution consultants.

Admission Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree in related field
  • Minimum 3 years of professional experience
  • Strong academic record
  • Research proposal
  • Writing sample
  • Three letters of recommendation
Tuition Fees

Full programme: $6,800 CAD (Canadian Dollars). Research grants and assistantships available.

Post Doctoral Experience

3-12 Months Research

Put your research into practice as a visiting scholar. Work with our research team to develop your thesis into publishable papers or pursue new research topics.

Format On-campus
Research Focus Social Sciences
Duration Flexible

Programme Overview

The Post Doctoral Experience programme offers recent PhD graduates the opportunity to continue their research in a supportive academic environment.

Research Areas
  • Alternative Dispute Resolution and Conflict Studies
  • Social Justice and Equity Research
  • Educational Policy and Reform
  • International Development Studies
  • Organizational Behavior and Management
  • Cross-cultural Communication Studies
Programme Features
  • Access to research facilities and resources
  • Mentorship from experienced faculty members
  • Opportunities for collaborative research
  • Workshops on academic publishing and grant writing
  • Networking with other researchers and scholars
  • Participation in seminars and conferences
Expected Outcomes
  • Publication of research in peer-reviewed journals
  • Development of research proposals for external funding
  • Enhanced academic profile and CV
  • Presentation of research at conferences
  • Establishment of professional networks
Admission Requirements
  • PhD degree in a relevant field
  • Strong research record and publications
  • Detailed research proposal
  • Alignment of research interests with faculty expertise
  • Letters of recommendation from academic supervisors
Programme Fees

Monthly fee: $1,200 CAD. Includes access to facilities, mentorship, and resources.

Postgraduate Certificate in Education (PGCE)

1 Year Certificate

Prepares candidates to teach in various educational settings: secondary, postsecondary and industry with specialized pathways.

Format On-campus/Virtual
Specializations Multiple Pathways
Credits 30 ECTS

Programme Overview

The Postgraduate Certificate in Education (PGCE) is a professional teaching qualification that prepares graduates for careers in education.

Specialization Pathways
  • Secondary Education (Ages 11-18)
  • Postsecondary and Adult Education
  • Industry and Corporate Training
  • English Language Teaching (ELT)
  • Special Educational Needs (SEN)
  • International Education and ESL
Curriculum
  • Foundations of Education and Learning Theories
  • Curriculum Development and Instructional Design
  • Assessment and Evaluation in Education
  • Classroom Management and Student Engagement
  • Educational Technology Integration
  • Differentiated Instruction and Inclusive Practices
  • Professional Practice and Teaching Practicum
  • Educational Research and Reflective Practice
Learning Outcomes
  • Design and implement effective lesson plans
  • Apply diverse teaching strategies for different learners
  • Assess student learning using multiple methods
  • Create inclusive and supportive learning environments
  • Integrate technology effectively in teaching
  • Reflect on and improve teaching practice
  • Collaborate with colleagues and stakeholders
Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as certified teachers, instructors, corporate trainers, educational consultants, curriculum developers, and instructional designers.

Admission Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 or equivalent
  • Proficiency in English (IELTS 7.0 or equivalent for non-native speakers)
  • Statement of teaching philosophy
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Interview with programme faculty
  • Clear criminal record check
Tuition Fees

Full programme: $6,200 CAD (Canadian Dollars). Includes teaching practicum supervision.

Executive Diploma in Management (EDM)

1 Year Diploma

Develop executive expertise in modern managerial practice with specializations in Marketing, HR, Logistics, or Supply Chain Management.

Format On-campus/Virtual
Specializations Marketing, HR, Logistics, Supply Chain
Credits 30 ECTS

Programme Overview

The Executive Diploma in Management provides mid-career professionals with advanced management skills and specialized knowledge in key business areas.

Specialization Options
  • Marketing Management: Digital marketing, brand strategy, consumer behavior, marketing analytics
  • Human Resources Management: Talent management, organizational development, compensation strategies, HR analytics
  • Logistics and Operations Management: Supply chain optimization, operations strategy, quality management, logistics technology
  • Supply Chain Management: Procurement strategies, inventory management, distribution networks, sustainable supply chains
Core Curriculum
  • Strategic Management and Leadership
  • Financial Management for Executives
  • Organizational Behavior and Change Management
  • Business Analytics and Decision Making
  • Ethical Leadership and Corporate Governance
  • Innovation and Entrepreneurship
  • Global Business Environment
  • Capstone Project: Strategic Business Initiative
Learning Outcomes
  • Develop and implement business strategies
  • Lead organizational change and development
  • Apply specialized knowledge in chosen business area
  • Analyze business problems using data and analytics
  • Make ethical business decisions
  • Manage cross-functional teams effectively
  • Navigate global business challenges

Admission Requirements

  • Minimum 5 years of professional work experience
