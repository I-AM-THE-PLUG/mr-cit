About City Institute of Higher Learning

At City Institute of Higher Learning (C-IHL), we pride ourselves on fostering an environment of academic excellence and innovation. Established in 2013, C-IHL has quickly become a beacon of learning in Moncton, embracing both local and those studying virtually from abroad.

Our Story

City Institute of Higher Learning (C-IHL)

City Institute of Higher Learning (C-IHL) is an advanced research-focused education institute in New Brunswick, Canada, that serves the needs of the research community and businesses. C-IHL is part of the Institute of International Education Canada (IEC) Inc., founded in 2013.

The Institute is a significant place for scholarly research and business consultancy covering contemporary global issues. C-IHL focuses primarily on advanced research and management learning. We aim to produce graduates who are equipped with exceptional skills to serve local and international development needs.

Institute Timeline

Each phase of our history reflects our ongoing commitment to adapt and respond to the educational needs and aspirations of our community.

2013

Company Established

Institute of Chinese & Asian Research (ICAR)

In the early years, our journey began as the Institute of Chinese & Asian Research (ICAR), a hub dedicated to enhancing understanding and collaboration through focused research on Chinese and Asian studies.

2015

Institute of International Education Canada (IEC)

Expanding Global Horizons

As our scope expanded and our mission evolved to include a broader global perspective, we transitioned to the Institute of International Education Canada (IEC). This name change reflected our growing emphasis on offering a diverse range of international educational programs.

2024

City Institute of Higher Learning (C-IHL)

A New Era in Education

In 2024, we embraced a new identity as the City Institute of Higher Learning (C-IHL), signifying a comprehensive approach to higher education that merges research, practical knowledge, and innovative learning strategies.
